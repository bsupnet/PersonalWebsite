Invoice PDF Analyzer

Built a local analytics prototype that converts supplier invoice PDFs and images into sandwich ingredient cost, gross margin, and price trend insights.

Python Streamlit OCR Plotly
Key Tools

Streamlit, pandas, pdfplumber, OpenCV, Tesseract OCR, Plotly

Business Impact

App Visuals
Invoice Upload Line Item Parsing Margin Analysis

The app uses text extraction for PDFs and local OCR for image invoices, then applies deterministic parsing, item classification, ingredient alias mapping, and unit conversion logic. Matched invoice rows are rolled into sandwich recipes so users can compare ingredient cost, selling price, gross profit, gross margin, and component-level changes over time.
